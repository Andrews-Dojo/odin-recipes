Classic gaucamole dish

Guacamole is basically mashed, raw avocados mixed with other ingredients to taste. The avocados give guacamole the green base, and other ingredients, such as tomatoes and onions give it some textured flavor. Guacamole is especially popular on Super Bowl Sunday and Cinco de Mayo.

Ingredients

  • 4 Avocados
  • 1-2 Limes
  • 1/2 bunch of Cilantro
  • 1 large white onion
  • 3 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 Serano pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Slice avacodos in half. Remove seed. Scoop into large pyrex dish (discarding of peel)
  2. With potato masher or fork, mash avocados until desired texture. I prefer small chunks but other prefer completely smooth.
  3. Dice tomatoes. Add to dish
  4. Dice onion. Add to dish.
  5. Chop cilantro. Add to dish. I prefer to chop half the stalk ends off.
  6. Half lime. Squeeze over dish. You may need another lime - add at the end if needed. You can always add more lime but can't remove.
  7. With gloves, dice serano pepper and add to dish.
  8. Apply thin layer of salt across whole dish. To your taste.
  9. Stir all ingredients together with a fork, making sure all ingredients have been combined.
  10. Tase with preferred tortiall chips. Add additional salt, lime, and serano pepper as desired.
  11. For best results cover dish and put in refridgerator for 1-3hrs
  12. Serve to guests and reap their praises!
